Description

There’s no time to think. Aim. Shoot. Reload. Run. If you want to survive this zombie wasteland, you’ll have to think on your feet. Pick your character, choose your weapons, and ready yourself for wave after wave of flesh eating abominations! Welcome to Gunslinger: Zombie Survival – a top down, 3rd person shooter filled with zombie runners, blood fiends, and brutal bosses!

Features

  • Assault zombies with your 22-weapon arsenal
  • Roleplay with 35 different, playable zombie survivors
  • Experience 3 gameplay modes: Story, Survival, and Defense modes
  • Immerse yourself in a gorgeous, fully-3D Unity environment
  • Outwit, outrun, and outgun monsters as you survive the zombie apocalypse

About the Publisher

Parthenon Games and Pixel Frog are independent game developers and publishers based in Hong Kong, SAR. Parthenon Games and Pixel Frog collaborate to make free to play, micro-transaction based casual, midcore, and hardcore mobile gaming experiences -- for gamers, by gamers.
